March 12 at 3:27 PM ¬∑

Ang daming nagtatanong kung oversaturated na ba yung IT industry? My honest answer to this is NO especially dun sa may mga experience at skills na. You might disagree with me pero ang nakikita ko problem na kaya nila nasasabi oversaturated na is dumadami yung mga entry level na dev na hanggang basic lang at certification lang ang pang laban sa job hunting. Dati pwede pa yung pa basic basic lang with certification pero IBA na ngayon mabilis mag evolve ang IT industry at tumataas yung standard and expectation ng mga employer. So maybe we can say hindi oversaturated ang IT industry as a whole but only for entry level or jr level. Well ganun talaga eh kasi kung ang mindset nyo lang ay malaki sahod ng IT but you dont have the passion at yung effort na iupskill at maging competitive sa job hunting with your portfolio wala magfafail ka talaga. Imagine mga boss may 100(para masabi natin saturated) candidate then lahat kayo entry level tapos most of you graduate lang at may certification lang then may isa sainyo na maraming ginawang sample application na nasa portfolio nya tingin nyo sino makukuha sainyo kung pare-parehas lang kayong entry level with cerification and basic knowledge?
